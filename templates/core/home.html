Al-Juhany Foundation: We Stand Together, For A Better Society

"Al-Juhani Foundation for International Arbitration and Social and Sustainable Development is a non-profit organization that aims to promote social justice and sustainable development in local, regional and international communities. The Foundation was established in 2023 at the initiative of Mr. Ahmed Fouad Al-Juhani, one of the most prominent arbitration experts in the Arab region, and includes in its membership a group of arbitrators, mediators and researchers in the fields of arbitration and development. The Foundation implements projects and programs in areas such as education, health, environment, human rights, economic and cultural empowerment.",
The Foundation contributes to the resolution of disputes and disputes in peaceful and constructive ways through international arbitration, which is a legal process carried out by a neutral third party who issues a binding decision to the disputing parties. International arbitration is an effective, expeditious and fair means of settling commercial, investment, diplomatic or humanitarian disputes.
